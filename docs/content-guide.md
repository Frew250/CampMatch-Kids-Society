## One-liners

- **What we do (barbecue):** "We help kids whose families can't swing the cost get to
  summer camp — we cover fees, and we work with camps to open up free or discounted spots."
- **Positioning vs the marketplace:** CampMatch.ca says *"find the right camp."* The
  Society says *"make sure every kid gets one."*

## Headline options (warm, specific, title-case — one orange accent word)

- "Helping more kids get a summer that **sticks with them.**"
- "Every kid deserves a summer **worth remembering.**"
- (Accent word in `--orange`. NOT all-caps. NOT "Build a Better Tomorrow" generic.)

## Script eyebrow ideas (Caveat, small, warm)

- "Camp should be for every kid"
- "Big summers for little people"

## The honest "this is real" facts (use instead of stats)

Present tense, true today, no numbers:
1. **A board in place** — ready to incorporate as a BC nonprofit.
2. **Camps already offering spots.**
3. **Supporters already stepping up.**

## Calls to action (capture, not donate)

- Primary everywhere: **"Keep me posted"** (email capture; mailto for now — no backend).
- Segmented "Get involved": **I'm a parent** ("Tell us about your kid") · **I run a camp**
  ("Offer spots / partner") · **I want to help** ("Be first to know when you can give").
- Family path wording is dignity-first: *"Tell us about your kid,"* never "apply" / "qualify."

## Footer / legal line (must appear)

> "Camp Match Kids Society is a BC nonprofit (incorporating) — the charitable sibling of,
> but a separate organisation from, the for-profit CampMatch.ca. Donations and tax
> receipts will open once our charitable registration is complete."

## Words to avoid (dignity)

"low-income," "qualify," "eligible," "underprivileged," "needy," "the less fortunate,"
and any means-test phrasing at the front door.

## Placeholders to replace before real launch

- `hello@campmatchkidssociety.org` mailto links (use the live inbox).
- Swap stock photos for real consented camp photos.
- Replace `mailto:` capture with a real form/email service (Phase 2 / backend).
